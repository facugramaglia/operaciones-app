# Design System Overview

This document outlines the core design principles and specifications for our user interfaces, ensuring consistency and a cohesive brand experience.

## Theme Configuration

**Color Mode:** light

**Branding Palette:**
*   **Primary Color:** `#438453` (A fresh, verdant green, symbolizing growth and tranquility, used for primary actions and key brand elements.)
*   **Secondary Color:** `#637e66` (A muted, earthy green, providing a sophisticated complement to the primary, ideal for secondary elements and subtle accents.)
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#e48a9f` (A soft, warm rose, adding a touch of elegance and warmth, used for highlights, badges, or specific interactive states.)
*   **Neutral Color:** `#747873` (A balanced, cool grey, serving as a versatile background and text color, ensuring readability and harmony.)

## Typography

Our typography is designed for clarity and modern aesthetics, utilizing the versatile Inter typeface across all text elements.

*   **Headline Font:** Inter (Clean, modern, and highly legible, suitable for impactful headings.)
*   **Body Font:** Inter (Optimized for long-form reading, ensuring comfortable user experience.)
*   **Label Font:** Inter (Clear and concise for interactive elements and form labels.)

## UI Geometry

**Roundedness:** Moderate (2)
UI elements feature a moderate level of roundedness, striking a balance between modern softness and structural clarity. This approach provides a friendly yet professional aesthetic without being overly casual.

## Spacing

**Spacing:** Normal (2)
Our design employs a normal level of spacing between UI elements, creating a balanced visual hierarchy and ensuring sufficient breathing room without feeling sparse or overly dense. This standard spacing promotes readability and ease of interaction.
